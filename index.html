Interested in trying archery?

We are a small archery club based in the Peterhead area. We shoot outdoors at Longside airfield from May to September and indoors at the Peterhead community centre from October through to April.

In both cases the club meets on a Sunday from 12:00pm to 3:00pm and we offer drop-in sessions whereby you can come along and try the sport (up to three sessions) for free.

We would usually encourage beginners to come along for the indoor season as we have a better set up for beginners in the community centre.

Want to become a member?

We would be more than happy for you to join our Archery club. Simply come along to one of our sessions, and we can talk you through what the process is.

We will require you to fill out a membership form, which you can either download, print off, and fill in yourself, or there will be copies available when attending a session.

The only real stipulation here is that children who wish to join the club must be over the age of 8, and must be accompanied by a parent/guardian at all times.

Different Bow types

Within the club we have a range of experience, as well as bow types. Folks within the club shoot Barebow, Longbow, Recurve, and Compound.

Have questions? Ask on our Facebook Group

If you have questions, then feel free to join our Facebook Group, and ask your questions, and we will do our best to answer them.

Club Constitution

Like any functioning club, we have a set of rules that Club and Committee Members need to stick to. You can find all of that information in Club Constitution Document.
